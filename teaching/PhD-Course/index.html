Numerical Methods for Time-Dependent PDEs: Course Information

Prerequisites

  • Sobolev and Bochner spaces
  • Finite elements for elliptic problems

Topics

  • Parabolic and wave problems
  • A priori analysis of time-stepping schemes for parabolic and wave (first and second order formulation) problems
  • A posteriori error analysis for elliptic, parabolic, and wave problems
