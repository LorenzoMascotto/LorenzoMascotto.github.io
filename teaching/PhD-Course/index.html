Course Information

Numerical Methods for Time-Dependent PDEs

Course Title

Information on the PhD Course "Numerical Methods for Time-Dependent PDEs", A.Y. 2025-2026

Prerequisites

  • Sobolev and Bochner spaces
  • Finite elements for elliptic problems

Course Goals

  • Understanding of parabolic and wave problems
  • A priori analysis of time-stepping schemes for parabolic and wave problems (first and second order formulation)
  • A posteriori error analysis for elliptic, parabolic, and wave problems
