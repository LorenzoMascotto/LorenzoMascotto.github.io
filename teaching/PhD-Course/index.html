Course Information: Numerical Methods for Time-Dependent PDEs

PREREQUISITES

  • Sobolev and Bochner spaces
  • Finite elements for elliptic problems

GOALS

  • Understanding of parabolic and wave problems
  • A priori analysis of time-stepping schemes for parabolic and wave (in first and second order formulation) problems
  • A posteriori error analysis for elliptic, parabolic, and wave problems
